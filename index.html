Final Report

      
By capturing the city’s horizontal and vertical structures and recomposing them into layered visual collages, the project carries the distinctive color palette, texture, and tempo of each district. These structured yet fluid patterns evoke the city’s architectural rhythm and the aesthetic clarity of its lines.

The final output reimagines barrier grid animation not merely as a visual illusion, but as a poetic lens through which Hong Kong’s layered identity is revealed — from its flickering core to its breathing periphery.
